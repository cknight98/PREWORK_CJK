Khruangbin is an American rock band from Houston. Their music is unique and other-worldly. It feels like a blend of rock, soul, psychedelia, and funk. Their sound is reminiscent of Middle Eastern Funk among other influences. The members are extremely talented. Marko makes use of lesser known scales and skillful trills. Laura and DJ make up an impecably groovy rhythym section.
